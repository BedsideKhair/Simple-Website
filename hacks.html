Hacks

Hack #1: Put newspaper at the bottom of your bin to absorb food juice!

This may sound strange, but it actually works! If you're tired of your bin leaking gunk every time you throw your waste away, just put fold up an old newspaper and fold it into the bottom of the bin before you start re-using it. Your hands will thank you for it in no time!

Hack #2: Use a spring from an old pen to keep your charger from bending/breaking!

Don't you hate it when your charger gets bended to the point where it breaks? Then look no further with this life hack. You can use a spring from any pen to keep your charger safe from bending and breaking. What with how frustratingly easy is to tangle up and tear open your phone chargers, not only does this solution improve longevity, it also encourages recycling!

Hack #3: Put pancake mix in a (used) ketchup bottle to mitigate mess

As roomy as those bowls can be for containing leftover pancake mix, they're hardly ideal when it comes to pouring them into the frying pan. It will take a bit of care getting it into the bottle in the first place, but after that, you'll be making the perfect pancakes in no time flat!

Hack #4: Use nail polish to identify different keys!

Whether you have any lying around or happen to see some while idly browsing in the shops, by giving the key heads a little dip then letting them dry for a few minutes, not only will your keychain be looking more fabulous, but also so much more distinctive!

Hack #5: Use cellphone light underneath a water bottle to make a lantern that distributes light better than the direct beam!

Whether your phone's flashlight isn't cutting it, or you can't afford to waste too much power, keep a clear water bottle on hand. With the beam refracting through the reflective water, you'll be able to shine a lot more of your darker living areas, better than ever before!

Hack #6: Cut a hole on the top corner of the juice box, so that it won't explode all over your table!

Got a spare toothpick? A clean knife? Anything sharp enough to poke through cardboard? If you've just come back from the grocery store with your favorite brand of juice, then use it to poke a tiny hole through the corner opposite the lid and you can enjoy your juice mess-free!

Hack #7: Bounce batteries to see if they're good or bad!

This may sound strange but it actually works. if you drop a battery on the ground and if it bounces. It means it has ran out. If the battery does not bounce though. It means it still has power!

Hack #8: Use a clean dustpan to fill a container that doesn't fit in the sink!

Don't you hate it when you try to fill a container up but it doesn't fit in the sink? Then look no further with this. You can use a dustpan that fits in the sink and it can be used to fill your container up without having to try and fit the container into the sink!

Hack #9: If in need of an inexpensive cookbook holder, try using a pants hanger!

Do you need something to hang your cookbook up but you don't have the budget to buy a cookbook holder. With this hack you won't need to buy one! Instead you can use a pants hanger as an alternative to hang your cookbook up!
