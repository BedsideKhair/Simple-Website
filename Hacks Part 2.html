Hacks

Hack #10: Freeze grapes to chill white wine without watering it down

Do you ever get annoyed when your wine gets too watery after adding in ice and it melts? Then instead use frozen grapes as it will not only chills wine, it also does not melt which means the grapes won;t make your wine all watery. Use this hack today if you want to have a nice cold drink!

Hack #11: Almost finished your jar of Nutella? End it with ice cream!

Of course you could just use the last of it in a sandwhich. But if you want something more tastier, add ice cream into the jar instead! You could use the last of the nutella with Ice cream. Adding extra flavour into it!

Hack #12: Use finished toilet paper rolls to separate cords and prevent tangling

Don't you hate it when all of your cords gets tangled up with each other and always go through the trouble of untangling them all? Then check out this hack! If you have any finished toilet papers around, you can use them to seperates cords from each other and prevent them from tangling up!

Hack #13: Use a straw or knife to remove the stem from strawberries

Having a hard time removing the stem from strawberries? Then try this! you can use a straw or a knife to remove the stem! Just Insert it into the strawberry next to the stem cap. Angle it towards the center core of the berry. Turn the strawberry to guide the straw/knife around the stem-cap and yes! the stem has been removed!

Hack #14: Place your smartphone in a cup to increase alarm volume

Some people may not be able to hear their own alarms go off and may not be able to turn it up louder. But with this hack you won't have this problem anymore. You can just put your phone into the cup as it will increase the volume for you!

Hack #15: Take pictures of friends holding items you've lent them, so you can remember them later

Do you ever lent items to your friends but forgotten about it? This hack is simple, just take a picture of your friends with the items or write down a list of your friends with your items so you can remember them later!

Hack #16: Tie a small, loose knot near the bottom tip of one headphone to more quickly differentiate between left and right

Ever get sick of putting on your headphones the wrong way? Having a hard time figuring out which is left and which is right? Then try out this hack! You can tie a small, loose knot near the bottom tip of one headphone. That way you will have a easy time to differentiate between left and right!

Hack #17: Stack clothes vertically in drawers to see them all more clearly

Annoyed that you have trouble pulling clothes out of your drawer? You should instead stack them vertically! that way it would be more easy for you to take them out!

Hack #18: Use bread clips to label and organize power cords

Have a hard time figuring out which power cord is plugged to? Then this hack is perfect for you! You can use bread clips to label and organize power cords!
