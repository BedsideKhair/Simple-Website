Hack #19: Use a hair clip to organize long wires

Another way to organize long wires is by using hair clips!

Hack #20: A frozen, saturated sponge in a bag makes an icepack that won't drip all over when it melts

Tired of Icepacks melting? Then you are in luck! With this hack, You can frozen up a saturated and then leave it in a bag which of course becomes a icepack but this time it won't melt!

Hack #21: Use a bread clip to hold a spot on a roll of tape

Having a hard time holding a roll of tape in place? You can instead use a bread clip to hold it in place for you!

Hack #22: When heating leftovers, space out a circle in the middle so that it will heat up much more evenly

Having leftovers and putting them in the microwave? You can space them out from the center so that your leftovers will heat up evenly!

Hack #23: Tie a small piece of brightly-colored fabric to your luggage to save time in checking for your bags

Ever worried you might lose your luggage? Then with this hack you will find them in no time! You can tie a small piece of brightly-colored fabric to your luggage so that you will have easier time finding them and save more time checking in for them!

Hack #24: Take a picture of business cards people hand you in case you lose them

Ever panicked before because you lost your business cards? then have no fear! You can instead take a picture of your business cards as a backup plan just in case you do lose them!

Hack #25: Doritos are a great substitute for kindling if you can't find any

This may sound strange but it actually works! If need something to light up fire more easily? You can actually use Doritos as a good alternative for great substitute for starting up fires. This might be because doritos have powdered seasoning over them which are flamable!
